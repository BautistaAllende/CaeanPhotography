Work

As a sports photographer, I specialize in capturing the dynamic and intense moments of sporting events. With a keen eye for action and timing, I aim to convey the passion and athleticism through visually compelling images. I work closely with clients to meet their specific needs and deliver high-quality photographs that immortalize the excitement of sports.

Additionally, I prioritize professionalism and collaboration, working closely with event organizers, athletes, and teams to ensure that their specific photographic needs are met. I am adept at capturing both individual performances and the collective spirit of teams, creating a comprehensive visual narrative of the sporting event.

About Caean

Caean is an alumni of the University of Washington, where he first gained a passion for photography while working for the student newspaper, The Daily, where he served as Photo Editor and became a 4th place finalist for the Associated Collegiate Press’ 2018 Photo of the Year Award in the General News category.

Alongside his position at The Daily, Caean covered the 2018 Seattle Seahawks season for the NFL.

Since leaving The Daily in early 2019, subsequently graduating that June, Caean has been a full-time freelance photographer primarily covering sports in the city of Seattle. His clientele include: The Associated Press, Seattle Sounders FC, OL Reign FC, Charlotte FC, Real Salt Lake, University of Washington, and Seattle University.

His life’s mission is to make images that will deeply resonate with their audience, and document the period of time in which he lives.
